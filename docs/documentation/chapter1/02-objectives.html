Chapter I

Objectives of the Study

General Objective

To develop Knowing Eye: A Web-Based Examination Platform with Behavior Monitoring Using Facial and Postural Analysis to enhance examination integrity, monitoring efficiency, and assessment reliability.

Specific Objectives

  1. Develop a centralized web-based examination platform capable of managing exam creation, administration, and monitoring within a unified system.
  2. Develop a monitoring and data management system that captures and stores examination-related data.
  3. Apply computer vision techniques to analyze examinee behavior in real time:
    1. Detect facial presence and identity consistency
    2. Track head movement, eye gaze, and posture
    3. Identify abnormal or suspicious behavioral patterns
  4. Integrate deep learning algorithms:
    1. Utilize YOLO for face and posture detection
    2. Apply CNN for feature extraction
    3. Implement FaceNet or ArcFace for facial feature consistency verification
  5. Develop a behavior monitoring and scoring module that flags anomalies, generates behavioral indicators, and supports administrator review.
  6. Generate an administrative dashboard for reviewing sessions, analyzing behavioral reports, and supporting integrity decisions.
  7. Evaluate the functionality, usability, and effectiveness of the platform in improving monitoring efficiency and supporting fair assessment.
