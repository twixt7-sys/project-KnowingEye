System Planning

The planning phase establishes the project team, decomposes the work, and schedules delivery. These artifacts are maintained as live, editable data in the Project OS and seeded from version-controlled JSON.

Project Team Organization

The development team is composed of four members under the guidance of a project adviser. Roles are assigned by competency across backend, frontend, AI/computer-vision, and integration/documentation responsibilities.
