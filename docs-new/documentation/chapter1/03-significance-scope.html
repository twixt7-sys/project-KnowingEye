Chapter I

Significance, Scope & Limitation

Significance of the Study

  • Educational institution - improves examination integrity and monitoring efficiency through automated behavior monitoring and centralized management.
  • Examination proctors - assists in detecting and recording suspicious behaviors, reducing manual supervision workload.
  • Instructors - provides behavioral reports supporting fair and transparent evaluation.
  • Students - promotes a secure and fair examination environment through consistent monitoring.
  • Future researchers - reference for web-based examination systems using computer vision and AI.

Scope

Development of Knowing Eye at Legacy College of Compostela. Data includes webcam video streams during examination sessions, system-generated behavioral logs, examination interaction records, and datasets (public and consented mock sessions) for model training and evaluation.

Included: Web-based exam management and delivery; role-based login; session tracking and response submission; behavior monitoring with webcam analysis; session logs, reports, and alerts; administrative dashboard and profile management.

Limitation

Limited to institutional web-based examination on desktop/laptop with webcams. Mobile deployment, external SIS integration, specialized biometric hardware, nationwide deployment, and offline operation are excluded. The system provides behavioral indicators - final judgment remains with administrators.
